ABOUT US
Nanhe Pakshi NGO, founded in August 2018 by Ms. Pallavi Kashyap and Mr. Shubham Sharma, is a social work platform whose main motto is to bring lost psitiveness in the society through the tools of better education for all along side having a healthy hygiene. We started with a single small scale project with the idea of free basic education for all with now organizing many big scale projects with core focus on three sectors namely education, hygiene and personality development of an individual. Our project Paathshaala solely focus on educating the needy whether children or adult. Our project Unnati focusses on different social issues and work on them with idea of improvement of hygiene through events such as heath check ups, cleanliness drives, educating on women health, donation of proper material, and focuses on grooming of an individual through various regularly organized events such as self defense training, mentoring on individual basis, women empowernment and many more. We believe in the innovative ideas of our young generation force and we thus compirse majorly of them. The environment we work is completely open to ideas, innovative people, positive thoughts, courageous nature and the will to change and be a change.
